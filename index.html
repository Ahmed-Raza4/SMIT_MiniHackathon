Roti Bank

One of the masterpieces of Saylani Welfare International Trust is Saylani Roti Bank. Poor people can eat from our Dastarkhwan but how will they feed their wives and children? To this end, the Saylani Welfare International Trust has set up a department called Saylani Roti Bank from where food can be procured and taken home without any discrimination on the basis of ethnicity or color. Even the children of the present poor can eat to their heart's content. This food is packed in beautiful packaging.
